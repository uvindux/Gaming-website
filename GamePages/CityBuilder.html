City Builder

Simulation / Strategy

Released: March 15, 2025

¬£3.99

A city-building simulation game where players create and manage their city.

Publisher: S.Mehedi Games

Game Description

An epic battle scene from Fantasy Quest featuring a knight with a glowing blue sword facing a large dragon in a fiery forest, illuminated by beams of light.

Featuring stunning graphics, an engaging storyline, and a complex character progression system, this game offers over 100 hours of gameplay with multiple endings based on your choices. Explore ancient ruins, battle fearsome creatures, and unlock powerful abilities as you embark on an unforgettable adventure.

Game Instructions

  • Use WASD keys to move your character around the world.
  • Press SPACE to jump and SHIFT to sprint.
  • Left-click to attack with your equipped weapon.
  • Press E to interact with objects and NPCs in the world.
  • Open your inventory by pressing I and the map by pressing M.
  • Save your game progress by resting at any campfire or safe house.

Tips & Strategies

  • Focus on completing side quests early to level up before tackling main story missions.
  • Collect herbs and minerals throughout your journey to craft powerful potions and equipment.
  • Different enemies have unique weaknesses - experiment with various weapon types to find the most effective approach.
  • Upgrade your favorite weapons at blacksmiths in major towns to increase their damage output.
  • Join a faction early in the game to unlock special abilities and exclusive quests.
